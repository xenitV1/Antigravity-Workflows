# 2. Google Business Profile

## 2.1 Required Information

```markdown
‚òê Accurate and consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
‚òê Business category selected
‚òê Business hours current
‚òê Website URL
‚òê Service areas specified
‚òê High-quality photos (min. 10)
‚òê Business description (keyword-rich but natural)
```

## 2.2 Advanced Features

```markdown
‚òê Regular Google Posts (2-3 per week)
‚òê Product/Service catalog added
‚òê Messaging enabled
‚òê Q&A section managed
‚òê Booking button (if applicable)
‚òê Attributes selected
‚òê Regular responses to customer reviews
```

## 2.3 GBP Optimization Tips

```markdown
1. Photo Strategy
   - Interior photos (min 5)
   - Exterior/storefront (2-3)
   - Product/service images (10+)
   - Team photos
   - Regular updates (2-3 new per month)

2. Google Posts
   - Share 2-3 posts per week
   - Promotions and special offers
   - Event announcements
   - Share blog posts
   - Add CTA button

3. Review Management
   - Actively request customer reviews
   - Respond to ALL reviews (positive and negative)
   - Professional response to negative reviews
   - Prepare review templates

4. Q&A Section
   - Pre-answer frequently asked questions
   - Check questions weekly
   - Write keyword-rich answers
```

# 4. Local Link Building

## 4.1 Local Link Strategies

```markdown
1. Local Press and News Sites
   - Local newspaper sponsorships
   - Press releases
   - Collaboration with regional bloggers

2. Local Partnership & Sponsorships
   - Neighborhood events
   - Sports clubs
   - Local associations
   - School sponsorships

3. Local Business Directories
   - Chamber of commerce membership
   - Trade association listings
   - Professional organizations

4. Community Engagement
   - Guest posts on local blogs
   - Active in neighborhood forums
   - Local social media groups
```

## 4.2 Local Content Ideas

