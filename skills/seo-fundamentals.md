# 2. E-E-A-T Principles

## 2.1 What is E-E-A-T?

**E-E-A-T**: Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness

Google's core framework for evaluating content quality.

## 2.2 E-E-A-T Criteria

### Experience
```markdown
‚úÖ Personal experience and observations
‚úÖ Real user stories and case studies
‚úÖ Practical application examples
‚úÖ Original visual and video content
```

### Expertise
```markdown
‚úÖ Clear author information and bio
‚úÖ Certifications and educational background
‚úÖ Industry experience stated
‚úÖ Expert opinions and quotes
```

### Authoritativeness
```markdown
‚úÖ Backlinks from recognized industry sources
‚úÖ Media mentions and interviews
‚úÖ Conference speeches and publications
‚úÖ Social proof
```

### Trustworthiness
```markdown
‚úÖ HTTPS secure connection
‚úÖ Current and accurate information
‚úÖ Transparent contact information
‚úÖ Privacy policy and terms
‚úÖ User reviews and ratings
```

## 2.3 E-E-A-T Improvement Strategies

```markdown
1. Enhance Author Profiles
   - LinkedIn, Twitter profile links
   - "About the Author" sections
   - Create author page/biography

2. Use Sources and Citations
   - Scientific articles and research
   - Industry reports
   - Expert opinions

3. Add Schema Markup
   - Author schema
   - Organization schema
   - Article schema
   - Review schema

4. Strengthen Trust Signals
   - SSL certificate
   - Contact page
   - Customer testimonials
   - Awards and certifications
```
